The Glorious History of Maratha Empire

Introduction

The Maratha Empire was one of the largest and most significant empires in Indian history, established by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in 1674.

Timeline

  • 1630: Birth of Shivaji Maharaj

  • 1680: Death of Shivaji Maharaj
  • 1689-1700: Rise of Maratha power under Rajaram and Tarabai
  • 1713-1740: Peak under Peshwa Bajirao I
  • 1761: Third Battle of Panipat
  • 1818: Decline and British conquest
